DEMO SCRIPT
===========

Short script for a 1-2 minute demo video (Loom or similar):

1. Show products page — 5s
- Explain the app is called Ecom and you have products seeded.

2. Add a couple of items to the cart — 10s

3. Open cart and change quantity/remove an item — 15s

4. Proceed to checkout, fill name/email and complete purchase — 15s

5. Show receipt and explain backend endpoints briefly — 20s

That's it — total ~1 minute. Save screenshots to `screenshots/`.
